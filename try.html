"Legim-Days"
"The word Lezim is a Sanskrit word, which means Beauty. With its beatiful pattern of sound and music, the dance attracts large number of people. It is combination of physical excercise and art of dancing. The accompanying instruments is small mallet and drums."
"Miravnuk Dance"
"The last day of the Ganpati festival is full of fun, dance and music as the Ganesh idol are taken out from the pandals. Ganesh Visarjan proceedings include large processions of avid Ganpati Bhakts dancing to loud bands. Miravnuk orchestrated performances with dhol pashas and lezim troupes and the famous tunes of Nashik Dhol. It is excited to take part in the procession and the bid adieu to Lord Ganesha with the hopes of welcoming him once again next year."
"9- Days of Colors"
" Navratri occurs over 9 days during the month of Ashvin, it often ends with the Dussehra. It is a time for dancing and feasting. Dances performed include garba, especially in Gujarat. Typically, the festival’s nine nights are dedicated to different aspects of the divine feminine principle, or shakti. Navratri is full of Garba and cheerful celebrations as it is the celebration of divinity!"
