Dance-ma-Taaz: "Dance is an adorn for my life.."

Hobby can be described as avocation,sport, pastime, amusement, entertainment. A hobby gives a pleasure as we do it for love of work and not under any complusion.

My favourite hobby is to dance. Dance is an adorn for my life. My mother is a dancer and my interest in dance always grew by seeing her dancing.

My mother was my first Guru. During my school annual gatherings, my mother used to choregraph my dance.

Even my friends and teachers were big fan of my mothers dance. Whenever there used to be any grand gatherings in my school, my mother used to choregraph all the dances.

My most favourite memory is the annual function of my junior kg class. At first I was a very shy person, i had stage-fever. In my annual function I took part in dance competition. It was the first time when I was going to perform on stage. I was at waiting at backstage with my mother and there was huge crowd of audience in hall. When my name was called on stage, I started to shiver. My mom helped me calm down and said just perform with full confidence.

The moment i started to walk towards the stage, I could hear audience shouting out my name, there were cameras and lights which were all focused on me. The song started, I went completely blank, forgot all steps and started to cry.

My teachers who were present at backstage supported me by saying dont feel nervous no one is going to laugh just be confident and dance, but there efforts were in vain.But, then this moment became the most memorable and unforgetable memory of my life when i was accompained by my mother. She came on stage, stood beside me and said softly I am here dont worry. With her presence I felt confident and we both spread the sweet chocolateness through our performance on the song "Asava sundar choclate cha Bangla."Though we were not the winners of this competition, but our performance stole heart of audience and this is how my interest in dance went on increasing.

Dancing fills my heart with joy. I can express myself through daance, it makes me feel free, at times just to forget my problems and enjoy the moment. For me dance is like a art, a best way to spend my free-time.

Dancing is not just a hobby for me, but it help me be fit and healthy because for me doing exercise is boring thing. It not only gives me confidence, but also shapes my fitness. Dance has no barriers of age, sex, religion Anybody and Everybody can dance. No matter how old you become, Just Dance it out. So, what are you waiting for? Turn on the music and dance it out!!..
